## Raised Bed — Left (facing horse farm)

| Plant | Confidence | Maintenance Notes |
|-------|-----------|-------------------|
| Pachysandra | Confirmed | Evergreen ground cover; minimal care, trim edges if spreading |
| Geranium | Confirmed | Deadhead spent blooms to encourage re-flowering |
| Astilbe | Less certain | Feathery plumes; prefers moist soil; cut back dead stalks in fall |
| Sedum | Confirmed | Drought tolerant; very low maintenance |
| Brunnera | Less certain | Shade-tolerant; heart-shaped leaves, blue spring flowers; keep moist |
| Hosta | Confirmed | Shade-tolerant; watch for slug damage; cut back in fall |
| Iris | Confirmed | Divide clumps every 3–4 years if blooming declines |
