## ⚠️ Find Your Specific Pickup Day

Different streets have different pickup days (Mon–Fri). Three ways to find yours:

1. **Recycle Coach app** (easiest) — download on iPhone or Android, enter your address. Township recommends keeping it updated.
2. **Service map** — https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/viewer?mid=12qo7Gc9XmkORfolfH07tCUWFf7VU-G8
3. **Call Whitetail** — 610-936-9967 (Mon–Fri 8am–5pm, Sat 8am–noon)

Once confirmed, update this file with your day and whether this week is a trash-only or trash+recycling week.

## Recycling — What's Accepted
✅ Plastics #1–7 (rinse clean)  
✅ Printed paper (not shredded, not wet)  
✅ Cardboard (broken down; not soiled)  
✅ Aluminum & tin cans (rinsed)  
✅ Glass bottles & jars (rinsed)  
✅ Pizza boxes (now accepted!)

❌ Plastic bags, wrap, or film  
❌ Flexible packaging (chip bags, pouches)  
❌ Styrofoam / packing peanuts  
❌ Shredded paper  
❌ Food or yard waste  
❌ Do NOT bag recyclables

## Bulk Waste
- Available weekly, but **must be scheduled in advance** — call 610-936-9967 or email by noon the day before pickup
- Furniture, carpet, mattresses, non-freon appliances accepted
- Mattresses must be in plastic; toilets must be bagged
- No construction debris
